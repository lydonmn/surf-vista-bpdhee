
# 6K Video Upload - Quick Start Guide

## What You Need

### Hardware
- **Drone or camera** capable of 6K recording (e.g., DJI Mavic 3, GoPro Hero 11)
- **iPhone or iPad** with sufficient storage space
- **Fast WiFi connection** (minimum 10 Mbps upload speed)

### Software
- SurfVista app installed
- Admin account access
- Supabase Pro tier subscription (required for 2GB file uploads)

## Recording Your 6K Video

### Recommended Settings
- **Resolution**: 6K (6144x3160) or higher
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Duration**: Under 90 seconds (strictly enforced)
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 or H.265)
- **Bitrate**: 100-150 Mbps

### DJI Drone Settings
1. Open DJI Fly app
2. Go to Camera Settings
3. Select Video Mode
4. Set Resolution: 6K
5. Set Frame Rate: 30fps or 60fps
6. Record your surf report video (keep under 90 seconds)

### GoPro Settings
1. Open GoPro app
2. Go to Video Settings
3. Select Resolution: 5.3K or higher
4. Set Frame Rate: 30fps or 60fps
5. Record your surf report video (keep under 90 seconds)

## Uploading Your Video

1. **Transfer Video to iPhone**

## Troubleshooting

### "Resolution too low"
**Problem**: Video is not 6K resolution

**Solutions**:
- Check camera settings - ensure 6K mode is selected
- Re-record video at 6K resolution
- Use a camera capable of 6K recording

### "Duration too long"
**Problem**: Video exceeds 90 seconds

**Solutions**:
- Trim video to under 90 seconds using Photos app
- Re-record a shorter video
- Use video editing app to cut down length

### "File too large"
**Problem**: Video file exceeds 2 GB

**Solutions**:
- Reduce video duration
- Lower bitrate in camera settings
- Compress video using video editing app
- Record at 6K 30fps instead of 60fps

### "Could not read video information"
**Problem**: Video format not supported

**Solutions**:
- Convert video to MP4 format
- Use H.264 codec instead of H.265
- Re-export video from editing app

### Upload Fails or Stalls
**Problem**: Network issues during upload

**Solutions**:
- Ensure strong WiFi signal
- Close other apps using bandwidth
- Try uploading during off-peak hours
- Restart router if needed
- Try different WiFi network

### Upload Takes Too Long
**Problem**: Upload is very slow

**Solutions**:
- Check WiFi upload speed (should be 10+ Mbps)
- Move closer to WiFi router
- Disconnect other devices from WiFi
- Upload during off-peak hours
- Consider compressing video slightly

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I upload 4K video?**
A: No, minimum resolution is 6K.

**Q: Can I upload longer than 90 seconds?**
A: No, maximum duration is strictly 90 seconds.

**Q: Why does upload take so long?**
A: 6K videos are very large files (1-2 GB). Upload time depends on WiFi speed.

**Q: Can I upload on cellular data?**
A: Not recommended. Use WiFi for reliable uploads.

**Q: What if upload fails?**
A: Check WiFi connection and try again. Video is not lost.

**Q: Can I edit video after upload?**
A: Not currently. You'll need to upload a new version.
